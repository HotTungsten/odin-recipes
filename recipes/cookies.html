Triple Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Description

There is nothing more to say other than the fact that these are triple chocolate chip chunk cookies. The name itself is explanatory, but these cookies are absolutely worth the time and effort it takes to make them. Consider the following: You do not have triple chocolate chip chunk cookies. By perusing and inevitably following this recipe, you too could now possess triple chocolate chip chunk cookies.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
