Classic Waffles

Description

Some delicious waffles for breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  1. Mix together the ingredients
  2. Beat the eggs and stir in the ingredients
  3. Cook em
